about

At 35 years old, my journey is only beginning. I found my passion for developing creative work much later than your typical new age developer. But, this hasn't slowed me down. Although it's a massive challenge, the people I love and care for (wife and 2 year old son) are dependent upon my success.

Upon completion of my Udemy basic front end course, my fingers never left my keyboard. Whether it was good code, bad code, or even great code (to me atleast), my mind was focused and inspired by this great community of devs pushing me on. HTML - Got it!. CSS - fighting with it daily, but I think I got it!. Javascript - const gotIt = long story, but I got it; But, you have my github...You tell me?
